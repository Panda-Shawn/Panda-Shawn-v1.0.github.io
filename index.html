I am currently a master student at Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Xueqian Wang. During my master's degree, I also worked as an intern at Tencent AI Lab, collaborating with Dr. Liu Liu and Dr. Peilin Zhao. Prior to that, I completed my bachelor's degree at School of Mechatronics and Engineering, Harbin Institute of Technology. Recently I have been working as a research assistant at Prof. Lin Shao's Lab and I am going to pursue my PhD degree under his supervision at National University of Singapore.

Research interest

I am broadly interested in research on the generalization and adaptation of RL agents.
